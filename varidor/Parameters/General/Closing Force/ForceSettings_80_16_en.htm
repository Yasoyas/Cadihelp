Force Settings

Summary

 This section describes the parameters in the parameter window Force Settings of the parameter group Closing Force in subsystem General.

Max Closing Force

The maximum force exceeded on an obstacle during closing in N.
The maximum force value is limited in compliance with safety code standard.
EN max. 150 N
ANSI max. 133 N
CSA max. 135 N

 














CFO Delay


Delay of the CFO filter in s, Bigger values make the CFO more robust
and less sensitive





















Closing Force Raw


Closing force selector





















Closing Force Blind Zone


ClosingForceBlindZone is the Zone near the closed position where the Closing Force Observation is switched off. It is measured from the ClosedPosition.
ATTENTION!
Setting the ClosingForceBlindZone too big causes severe risk of injury of passengers hands! Adjust to the smallest possible value!
