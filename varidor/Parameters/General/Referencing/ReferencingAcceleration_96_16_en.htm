Referencing Acceleration

Summary

 This section describes the parameters in the parameter window Referencing Acceleration of the parameter group Referencing in subsystem General.

Refr Acc

RefrAcc is the acceleration used during referencing trips in m/s/s The acceleration is reduced automatically to approx. 0.1m/s^2, if the door parameters (especially mass) are unknown.

 














Refr Acc Stopping


RefrAccStopping is the Stopping acceleration used during referencing trips at a Stop command in m/s/s
This Acceleration should be big enough to stop from RefrSpeed within the RefrPosition1 distance 

RefrAccStopping > RefrSpeed    ^2      /2/RefrPosition1,



Besides that the acceleration should not be bigger than 0.1m/s^2,
