2080 E_TRIP_PHNR_SIGNAL_INCONSISTENT

Summary

 This section describes the 2000_2099_TRIP_MANAGER error E_TRIP_PHNR_SIGNAL_INCONSISTENT.

Description

After each landing of the car on a floor a consistency check of the shaft information signals for relevelling PHNR_U and PHNR_D gets performed. This error occurs if both of the signals are inactive at this point of time.
