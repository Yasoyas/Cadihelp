0025 E_ELEVATOR_ISK_PREOPEN_ERROR

Summary

 This section describes the 0000_0099_ELEVATOR error E_ELEVATOR_ISK_PREOPEN_ERROR.

Description

Safety circuit opened unexpected at tap 'ISK' during pre-opening of the doors.

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 A safety device has tripped Check why the corresponding safety device has tripped and resolve problem
2 Safety circuit wiring faulty or safety circuit contact(s) defective or bad adjusted Check general wiring safety circuit and safety circuit contacts at corresponding safety circuit section
