0074 E_ELEVATOR_TRACTION_MEANS_TEMP_EXCEEDED

Summary

 This section describes the 0000_0099_ELEVATOR error E_ELEVATOR_TRACTION_MEANS_TEMP_EXCEEDED.

Description

The temperature of the elevator traction means (e.g. belts) has exceeded the allowable operating temperature

Extra Info

0: Unknown (not supported)
1: Overtemperature
2: Undertemperature

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Too hot ambient air temperature (temperature in hoistway) Wait for cool down
2 The temperature feedback signal (e.g. KTHS) is faulty Check general wiring to temperature sensor
3 The heat dissipation is not working Check operation of heat dissipation device (e.g. fan or forced ventilation) if present
