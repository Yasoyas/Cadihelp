0024 E_ELEVATOR_KNE_U_INTERRUPTED

Summary

 This section describes the 0000_0099_ELEVATOR error E_ELEVATOR_KNE_U_INTERRUPTED.

Description

Safety circuit opened unexpected (e.g. during trip) at tap 'KNE_U'.

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 The car has exceeded the hoistway end limit (e.g. KNE_U) Check why the car has exceeded the hoistway end limit.
Check log for possible previous reported messages.
Release elevator from blocked state (perform reset procedure).
2 Safety circuit wiring faulty or safety circuit contact(s) defective or bad adjusted Check general wiring safety circuit and safety circuit contacts at corresponding safety circuit section
