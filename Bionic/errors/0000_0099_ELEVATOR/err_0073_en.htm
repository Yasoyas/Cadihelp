0073 E_ELEVATOR_BACKUP_BAT_CAR_SUFFICIENT_CHARGE

Summary

 This section describes the 0000_0099_ELEVATOR error E_ELEVATOR_BACKUP_BAT_CAR_SUFFICIENT_CHARGE.

Description

The charge of the car backup battery (e.g. used to power the car backup light) has resumed to the required minimum level.
