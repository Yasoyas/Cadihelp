0053 E_ELEVATOR_RECALL_TRIP_DESTINATION_CHANGED

Summary

 This section describes the 0000_0099_ELEVATOR error E_ELEVATOR_RECALL_TRIP_DESTINATION_CHANGED.

Description

The destination of a running recall trip has changed (e.g. due to activation of a higher prioritized operating mode).

For more information see also current status (operating mode) of elevator.
