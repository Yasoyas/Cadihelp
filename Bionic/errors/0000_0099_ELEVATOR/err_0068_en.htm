0068 E_ELEVATOR_REDUCED_DRIVE_FAILURE_DETECT_STARTED

Summary

 This section describes the 0000_0099_ELEVATOR error E_ELEVATOR_REDUCED_DRIVE_FAILURE_DETECT_STARTED.

Description

The car may be moved in open loop mode with reduced failure detection on inspection or installation operation.
Disabled failure detection: Drive motor encoder failure, - Drive motor speed control deviation, Drive motor overload, Drive motor underload, Drive motor Overtemperature, Drive brake failure.
