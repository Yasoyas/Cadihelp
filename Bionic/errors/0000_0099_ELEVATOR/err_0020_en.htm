0020 E_ELEVATOR_S_CHAIN_BRIDGED_PERMANENT

Summary

 This section describes the 0000_0099_ELEVATOR error E_ELEVATOR_S_CHAIN_BRIDGED_PERMANENT.

Description

Safety circuit did not open when expected (e.g. while opening door)

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 The safety circuit is bridged (e.g. at the car or landing door) Check safety circuit for bridges (e.g. plugs) and remove them
