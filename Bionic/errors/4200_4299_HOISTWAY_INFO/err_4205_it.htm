4205 E_HI_END_DETECTION_MISMATCH_TRAVEL_DIRECTION

Summary

 This section describes the 4200_4299_HOISTWAY_INFO error E_HI_END_DETECTION_MISMATCH_TRAVEL_DIRECTION.

Description

The hoistway end detection does not match with the car travel direction.
The hoistway information indicates for instance the car entering the lower hoistway end while in fact the car was entering the upper hoistway end.

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 The hoistway end signals (e.g. KSE_U, KSE_D) are faulty Check general wiring of hoistway end sensors (e.g. interchanged wires)
2 Actual car travel direction is wrong Check parameters defining the car travel direction (e.g. CF16 , PA14 / CF16 , PA15) for correct setting
