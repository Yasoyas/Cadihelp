0315 E_DOOR_NOT_RECOVERABLE

Summary

 This section describes the 0300_0399_CAR_DOOR error E_DOOR_NOT_RECOVERABLE.

Description

The door wasn't able to recover from a door opening or door closing error (typ. after 20 repetitive opening and closing attempts). The elevator is blocked.

ndef

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Door has a problem causing repetitive 'door opening' or 'door closing' errors. Check why door cannot recover.
