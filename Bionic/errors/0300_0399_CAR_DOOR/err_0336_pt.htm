0336 E_DOOR_OVERTEMPERATURE

Summary

 This section describes the 0300_0399_CAR_DOOR error E_DOOR_OVERTEMPERATURE.

Description

The door drive (motor) has exceeded its operating temperature.

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Too intensive operation (e.g. too many door cycles per time unit) Wait for cool down
2 Too hot ambient air temperature (e.g. direct sunlight at glass shaft) Wait for cool down
3 The temperature feedback signal is faulty Check general wiring to temperature sensor.
Check operation of temperature sensor.

Field Feedback
ID Location Actions to be taken
1 Car Door / Shaft Door Verificar o motor de portas, verificar obstruções/obstáculos na zona da porta ou na ranhura
