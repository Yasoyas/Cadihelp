0314 E_DOOR_PRE_OPENING

Summary

 This section describes the 0300_0399_CAR_DOOR error E_DOOR_PRE_OPENING.

Description

There was an activation or deactivation failure of the door safety circuit bypass device while the elevator was intended to perform a door pre-opening.

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Wiring of door safety circuit bypass device faulty Check general wiring of door safety circuit bypass device
2 Door zone detection faulty Check door zone signals and sensors (e.g. PHSx, PHUETx).
Check position of door unlocking zone indicators in hoistway (e.g. vanes on same level)
3 Failure at door safety circuit (KTS, KTC) bypass device Replace PCB containing this bypass mechanism (e.g. SUET)
