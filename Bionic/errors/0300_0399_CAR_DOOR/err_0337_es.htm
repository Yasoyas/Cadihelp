0337 E_DOOR_SUET_BOARD_DISCONNECTED

Summary

 This section describes the 0300_0399_CAR_DOOR error E_DOOR_SUET_BOARD_DISCONNECTED.

Description

The door safety circuit bypass device is detected as disconnected.

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 The door safety circuit bypass device was disconnected by a user. Reconnect the safety circuit bypass device (e.g. SUET)
2 A door safety circuit bypass feedback signal (e.g. IUET, RFUET) is faulty Check general wiring at door safety circuit bypass device (e.g. SUET)
3 Failure at door safety circuit bypass device Replace corresponding device (e.g. SUET)
