0339 E_DOOR_SUET_ERROR2_OVERBRIDGING_LOST

Summary

 This section describes the 0300_0399_CAR_DOOR error E_DOOR_SUET_ERROR2_OVERBRIDGING_LOST.

Description

The elevator has recognized an unexpected deactivation of the door safety circuit bypass. Note, this bypass is activated during re-leveling or advanced door opening (pre-opening).

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Door zone detection faulty Check door zone signals and sensors (e.g. PHSx, PHUETx).
2 A door safety circuit bypass feedback signal (e.g. IUET, RFUET) is faulty Check general wiring at door safety circuit bypass device (e.g. SUET)
3 The car has unintentionally left the door zone while the door safety circuit was bypassed Check why the car has left the door zone
4 Failure at door safety circuit bypass device Replace corresponding device (e.g. SUET)
