2138 E_SHAFT_CAR_RELEVELING_ZONE_SIGNAL_INVALID

Summary

 This section describes the 2100_2099_SHAFT error E_SHAFT_CAR_RELEVELING_ZONE_SIGNAL_INVALID.

Description

The signal indicating the car releveling zone is invalid (e.g. inconsistent). Failure at hoistway information.

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 A car releveling zone indicator is missing or not at the correct position Check correct position of the corresponding indicators (e.g. alignement of magnets, vanes)
2 A car releveling zone sensor is defective Check functionality of corresponding sensor (e.g. PHNR_U, PHNR_D). Replace it if necessary.
3 A car releveling zone sensor signal is faulty Check general wiring of corresponding sensors (e.g. PHNR_U, PHNR_D).
Check at optical sensors (e.g. PHNR_U. PHNR_D) vanes and sensors for dirt and proper shielding from sunlight (e.g. at glass shaft).
4 Bad data transmission Some hoistway information system transfer their data via data bus. Check data bus (e.g CAN) for correct data transmission (e.g. bad shielding of data wire).
