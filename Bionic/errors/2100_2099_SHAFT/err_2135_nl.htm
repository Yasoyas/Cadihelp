2135 E_SHAFT_TSD_SIGNAL_CHANGE

Summary

 This section describes the 2100_2099_SHAFT error E_SHAFT_TSD_SIGNAL_CHANGE.

Description

The elevator has detected an unexpected change of the signal TSD (state of the presence of the temporary safety device).

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 The wiring at the signal TSD is faulty Check general wiring
2 The elevator main controller board was exchanged by a board from another elevator Perform the learning travel. At no success replace elevator main controller board (e.g. SCIC, SCPU) by a new one from the factory.
