9038 S_NO_OPERATION_DUE_TO_STOP_IN_CAR_FIREFIGHTER

Summary

 This section describes the 9000_9099_ELEVATOR_STATUS_1 error S_NO_OPERATION_DUE_TO_STOP_IN_CAR_FIREFIGHTER.

Description

The elevator is stopped due to pressed firefighter stop switch in the car

Note, while in firefighter operation the standard stop switch (if present) figures also as a firefighter stop switch.
