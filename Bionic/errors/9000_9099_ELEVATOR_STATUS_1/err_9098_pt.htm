9098 S_ELEVATOR_BREAKDOWN

Summary

 This section describes the 9000_9099_ELEVATOR_STATUS_1 error S_ELEVATOR_BREAKDOWN.

Description

The elevator is blocked, but could get unblocked by reset or by switching off and on the power supply of the elevator main controller
