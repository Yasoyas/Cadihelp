3157 E_AAT_KNEU_FAILED

Summary

 This section describes the 3100_3199_AAT error E_AAT_KNEU_FAILED.

Description

The acceptance test 'KNE_U' has failed, because the safety circuit did not open when the car has arrived at the shaft end.
