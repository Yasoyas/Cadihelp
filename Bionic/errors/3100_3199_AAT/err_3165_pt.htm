3165 E_AAT_CIB_TEST_SAFETY_CHAIN_NOT_OPENED

Summary

 This section describes the 3100_3199_AAT error E_AAT_CIB_TEST_SAFETY_CHAIN_NOT_OPENED.

Description

The acceptance test 'Car impact on Buffer' has failed, because the safety circuit did not open when the car has hit the buffer.

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 The safety circuit switch KP is not installed Install the safety circuit switch KP
2 The safety circuit switch KP didn't get hit Check the safety circuit switch KP position
3 Failure in safety circuit wiring Check safety circuit wiring
4 Safety circuit switch KP defect Replace the safety circuit switch KP
