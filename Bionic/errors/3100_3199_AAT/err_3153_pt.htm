3153 E_AAT_SGCE_FAILED

Summary

 This section describes the 3100_3199_AAT error E_AAT_SGCE_FAILED.

Description

The acceptance test 'Safety Gear Car Empty' failed, because the safety gear seems not to be engaged after the initiation of the test (typ. 2s after pressing the DBV button).
Note, during this acceptance test the safety circuit is closed all the time. A succesfull engagement of the safety gear gets detected by the drive (FC) monitoring the speed of the car.

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Mechanical problem at safety gear Check mechanics at safety gear. Check mechanics at safety gear governor including the solenoid.
2 Faulty electrical connection to solenoid at safety gear governor Check electrical wiring between solenoid at safety gear governor and the elevator main controller main board (SMIC).
3 Faulty output on the interface board (PCB) controlling the solenoid at the safety gear governor Replace interface board (SMIC)
