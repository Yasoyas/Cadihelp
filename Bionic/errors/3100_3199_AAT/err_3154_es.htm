3154 E_AAT_KNEU_CAR_NOT_AT_LDU_FLOOR

Summary

 This section describes the 3100_3199_AAT error E_AAT_KNEU_CAR_NOT_AT_LDU_FLOOR.

Description

The acceptance test 'KNE_U' was tried to get started by the fitter while the car was not standing on the expected floor (LDU floor).

Extra Info

ndef

Source ID

ndef

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 The car is not standing on the LDU floor (e.g. because another acceptance test got performed previously) Ensure the car is standing on the LDU floor before starting the acceptance test
