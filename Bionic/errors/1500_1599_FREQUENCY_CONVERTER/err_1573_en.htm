1573 E_FC_MECHANICAL_BRAKE_KB_PERSISTENT_FATAL

Summary

 This section describes the 1500_1599_FREQUENCY_CONVERTER error E_FC_MECHANICAL_BRAKE_KB_PERSISTENT_FATAL.

Description

Too many brake failures (KB) have occurred within a certain limit of trips (typically > 3failures/100trips). The elevator is blocked.

Check brake contact KB for dirt. Check general wiring KB.
