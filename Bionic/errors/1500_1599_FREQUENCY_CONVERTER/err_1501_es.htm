1501 E_FC_OVERCURRENT

Summary

 This section describes the 1500_1599_FREQUENCY_CONVERTER error E_FC_OVERCURRENT.

Description

The maximum current limit on one or more drive inverter output phases to the drive motor has exceeded

Extra Info

0: unknown (not supported)
1: Failure at phase 1
2: Failure at phase 2
3: Failure at phase 3

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Short circuit in the motor cables or motor windings Check power wires and connectors between drive inverter and drive motor.
Check motor for short circuit at windings.
2 Driving mechanics inhibited or blocked Check driving mechanics for blocking (e.g. gear at geared drive, oiling)
Check brake for proper opening.
3 Sudden heavy load increase Check for impermissible loading
4 Drive parameter setting Check drive parameter and compare with motor type and its data
5 Unsuitable drive motor Check drive motor for correct dimensioning in the elevator system
