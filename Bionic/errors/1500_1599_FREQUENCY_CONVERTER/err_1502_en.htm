1502 E_FC_OVERVOLTAGE

Summary

 This section describes the 1500_1599_FREQUENCY_CONVERTER error E_FC_OVERVOLTAGE.

Description

Overvoltage at the drive inverter internal DC link detected

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Deceleration is too high Adjust the deceleration (proposal 0.5m/s2)
2 High over-voltage spikes at mains Check mains voltage for disturbances and tolerances
3 Drive inverter electronics failure Replace drive inverter
