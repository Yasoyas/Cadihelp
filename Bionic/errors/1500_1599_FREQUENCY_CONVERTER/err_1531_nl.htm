1531 E_FC_ENCODER_PULSE_MISSING

Summary

 This section describes the 1500_1599_FREQUENCY_CONVERTER error E_FC_ENCODER_PULSE_MISSING.

Description

Invalid signal from the drive motor encoder

Extra Info

0: Unknown (not supported)
1: Failure at Channel A signal
2: Failure at Channel B signal
3: Failure at Channel A and B signal
4: Encoder reversed
5: Card is missing

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Encoder signal is faulty Check general wiring to the encoder
2 Encoder signal is noisy Check encoder signals for right termination (terminating resistor)
3 Encoder signal receiving stage defective Replace electronics (e.g. option board or even entire drive inverter) of corresponding device
4 Encoder defective Replace encoder
5 Drive Parameter set wrong Check drive parameter (e.g. nominal frequency, impulse ratio etc.)
6 The rotation direction signaled by the encoder (differential inputs) is contrary to the drive motor mains phase sequence Change encoder direction parameter (CF 16, PA 14)
