1575 E_FC_WRONG_PHNRx_SEQUENCE

Summary

 This section describes the 1500_1599_FREQUENCY_CONVERTER error E_FC_WRONG_PHNRx_SEQUENCE.

Description

The drive frequency converter received a inconsistent relevel zone signal sequence from the shaft information system (e.g. two times rising edge from PHNR_U or PHNR_D).

ndef

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Bad alignment of the PHNR_U/PHNR_D sensors Check positions of the PHNR_U/PHNR_D sensors
2 Dirt on vanes Clean the vanes in the hoistway
3 Bad signals through external light source Check proper shielding from sunlight (glass shaft)
4 Communication problem, EMC Check communication on CAN bus (SDIC, SCIC)
