1542 E_FC_TOO_HIGH_OR_TOO_LOW_SPEED_AT_TARGET_PHSx_RISI

Summary

 This section describes the 1500_1599_FREQUENCY_CONVERTER error E_FC_TOO_HIGH_OR_TOO_LOW_SPEED_AT_TARGET_PHSx_RISI.

Description

Too high respectively too low car speed detected while the car is landing on floor

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Malfunction of car position detection Check hoistway information system (e.g. vanes, PHS)
2 Bad balancing of car and counterweight Check balancing according instruction manual (TK)
3 Too high traction slip Check traction means for enough friction
4 Wear of traction means Check traction means for wear
