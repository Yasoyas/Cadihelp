0402 E_DRIVE_TRIP_TOO_LONG

Summary

 This section describes the 0400_0499_DRIVE error E_DRIVE_TRIP_TOO_LONG.

Description

The car hasn't reached the intended destination floor within the legal time limit (typ. 45s). This mechanism protects the driving mechanics in case of a blocked car. E.g. at traction elevator, no damage of the ropes. E.g. at hydraulic elevator, no damage of hydraulic jack/valves.

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Car blocked or moving too slowly Check for object in shaft blocking the car.
Check car for obstruction (too much friction at guide rails).
2 Drive blocked or turning too slowly Check if brake opens right.
Check parameter nominal speed at drive.
3 Shaft information signal(s) faulty Check shaft info wiring and it signals (PHS, KS/KS1, KSE/KSE_U/KSE_D, ..)

Field Feedback
ID Location Actions to be taken
1 Car / Shaft Comprobar por qué la cabina está bloqueada (quizás haya un obstáculo en el hueco)
2 Drive Comprobar por qué el motor está bloqueado o el freno no abre. Comprobar el cableado de freno
