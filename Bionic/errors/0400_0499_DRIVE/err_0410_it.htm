0410 E_DRIVE_SPEED_LIMIT_EXCEEDED_DURING_SAFETYCHAIN_BR

Summary

 This section describes the 0400_0499_DRIVE error E_DRIVE_SPEED_LIMIT_EXCEEDED_DURING_SAFETYCHAIN_BR.

Description

E_DRIVE_SPEED_LIMIT_EXCEEDED_DURING_SAFETYCHAIN_BRIDGED:

While the car is landing on a floor the signal indicating low speed (typically at v < 0.3m/s) coming from the drive frequency converter (open loop) is expected to change once from inactive to active state. The error occurs if this signal has changed once to active state and changes back again to inactive state (meaning: The car speed has increased during landing).
Note, the safety circuit at the door is overbridged at this point of time.

Note, the elevator can get blocked if this error appears too many times within a specific period of time (typ. > 3times in 1hour).
