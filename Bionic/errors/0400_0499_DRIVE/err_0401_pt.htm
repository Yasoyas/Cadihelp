0401 E_DRIVE_SAFETY_CHAIN_INTERRUPTED

Summary

 This section describes the 0400_0499_DRIVE error E_DRIVE_SAFETY_CHAIN_INTERRUPTED.

Description

Circuito de segurança abriu inesperadamente (por ex. durante a viagem)

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Any safety device has tripped (safety circuit opened) Check reason for tripping and resolve problem
2 Safety circuit wiring faulty or safety circuit contact(s) defective or bad adjusted Check general wiring safety circuit
and safety circuit contacts
