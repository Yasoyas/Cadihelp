0439 E_DRIVE_INVERTER_PE_TEMP_EXCEEDED

Summary

 This section describes the 0400_0499_DRIVE error E_DRIVE_INVERTER_PE_TEMP_EXCEEDED.

Description

The car drive inverter power electronics has exceeded its operating temperature

Extra Info

0: Unknown (not supported)
1: Overtemperature
2: Undertemperature

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 The heat dissipation is not working Check operation of heat dissipation device (e.g. fan or forced ventilation) if present
2 Too hot ambient air temperature (e.g. direct sunlight at glass shaft) Wait for cool down
3 The temperature feedback signal is faulty Check general wiring to temperature sensor.
Check operation of temperature sensor.
4 Too intensive operation (e.g. too many trips per time unit) Wait for cool down
5 Too cold ambient air temperature Check operation of heater if present
