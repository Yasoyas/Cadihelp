0408 E_DRIVE_NOT_READY_DURING_TRIP

Summary

 This section describes the 0400_0499_DRIVE error E_DRIVE_NOT_READY_DURING_TRIP.

Description

The drive (frequency converter, open loop) got technically unavailable while the car was moving.

Extra Info

ndef

Source ID

ndef

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Bad electrical connection from the drive frequency converter to the elevator main contactors connection board (MCCE board) Check corresponding cables and connectors (e.g. the drive ready signal)
2 General problem at the drive frequency converter Check the drive frequency converter local error log for detailed diagnostics. Replace the frequency converter if necessary.
