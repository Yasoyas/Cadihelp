0406 E_DRIVE_OVERTEMPERATURE

Summary

 This section describes the 0400_0499_DRIVE error E_DRIVE_OVERTEMPERATURE.

Description

Drive overtemperature detected

Extra Info

ndef

Source ID

ndef

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Drive motor, hydraulic pump, hydraulic oil, or hoistway overtemperature due too many trips per time unit or due to too hot ambient air temperature (e.g. direct sunlight at glass shaft) Wait for cool down
2 Drive overtemperature feedback signal KTHMH/KTHM is faulty Check thermal contacts KTHMH/KTHM for right operation. Check general wiring KTHMH/KTHM.
3 The ventilation (integrated fan at frequency converter or forced fan) is not working Check power suppply and mechanics of fan
