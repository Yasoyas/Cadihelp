0409 E_DRIVE_SPEED_LIMIT_EXCEEDED_IN_STANDBY

Summary

 This section describes the 0400_0499_DRIVE error E_DRIVE_SPEED_LIMIT_EXCEEDED_IN_STANDBY.

Description

Every time before starting a new trip a consistency check of the signal indicating low speed (typically at v < 0.3m/s) coming from the drive frequency converter (open loop) is performed. The error occurs if the signal is not active.

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Bad electrical connection from the drive frequency converter to the elevator main contactors connection board (MCCE board) Check corresponding cables and connectors (e.g. the drive speed limit signal)
2 Parameter 'speed limit' is set wrong Check parameter 'speed limit' at drive frequency converter
3 General problem at the drive frequency converter Check the drive frequency converter local error log for detailed diagnostics. Replace the frequency converter if necessary.
