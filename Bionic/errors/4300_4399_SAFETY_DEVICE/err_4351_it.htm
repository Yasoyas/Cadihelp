4351 E_SAFETY_DEVICE_CAR_LOW_SPEED_LIMIT_DETECTION

Summary

 This section describes the 4300_4399_SAFETY_DEVICE error E_SAFETY_DEVICE_CAR_LOW_SPEED_LIMIT_DETECTION.

Description

The car low speed limit exceeded detection device is not working as expected. Its low speed signal does not correspond with the car speed supplied by the car drive.

Note, this inconsistency check is running while the car is not in maintenance operation (e.g. inspection).

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Low speed signal is faulty Check corresponding wiring
2 Failure at car low speed limit exceeded detection device Replace corresponding device (e.g. PCB)
