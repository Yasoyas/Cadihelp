4392 E_SAFETY_DEVICE_BYPASS_DOOR_CLOSED_DETECTION

Summary

 This section describes the 4300_4399_SAFETY_DEVICE error E_SAFETY_DEVICE_BYPASS_DOOR_CLOSED_DETECTION.

Description

There was an activation or deactivation failure of the bypass of the car door closed detection device and the landing door closed detection device.

Note, this bypass is only activated while the car is intended to be moved with open door(s). E.g. at door pre-opening while entering a floor or at car re-levelling.

Extra Info

0: Unknown (not supported)
1: Bypass activation failure (e.g. safety circuit not bridged)
2: Bypass deactivation failure (e.g. safety circuit bridged)
3: Bypass device failure (e.g. implausible activation state)
4: Door unlocking zone detection failure (e.g faulty signal)

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Failure at device which bypasses the car door closed detection (e.g. KTC) and the landing door locked detection (e.g. KTS) Replace PCB containing this bypass mechanism (e.g. CANCNA)
2 Door safety circuit wiring faulty Check general wiring safety circuit
3 Door unlocking zone detection faulty Check door unlocking zone signals and sensors (e.g. PHSx, PHUETx)
