4305 E_SAFETY_DEVICE_CAR_UNINTENDED_MOVEMENT

Summary

 This section describes the 4300_4399_SAFETY_DEVICE error E_SAFETY_DEVICE_CAR_UNINTENDED_MOVEMENT.

Description

A elevator safety device has detected that the car has performed a uncontrolled movement (e.g left the door zone with door open).

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Serious failure at traction means or brake Check carefully reason for unintended car movement and resolve problem.
Release elevator from blocked state by dedicated reset of the tripped safety device.
