1120 E_LMS_NO_SIGNAL

Summary

 This section describes the 1100_1199_CAR_LOAD_CELL error E_LMS_NO_SIGNAL.

Description

No signal from the car load measuring device

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 No connection to the car load measuring device Check connections and general wiring to the car load measuring device (e.g. CLC)
2 Malfunction of the car load measuring device Replace the car load measuring device (e.g. CLC)
