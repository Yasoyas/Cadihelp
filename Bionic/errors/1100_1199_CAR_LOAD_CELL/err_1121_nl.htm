1121 E_LMS_INVALID_SIGNAL

Summary

 This section describes the 1100_1199_CAR_LOAD_CELL error E_LMS_INVALID_SIGNAL.

Description

The signal from the car load measuring device is present but invalid (e.g. signal out of range)

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 The car load measuring device is mechanically bad adjusted Check mechanical fixation of the car load measuring device
2 The car load measuring signal is faulty Check general wiring to the car load measuring device.
Check for EMC disturbances.
3 Malfunction of the car load measuring device Replace the car load measuring device (e.g. CLC)
