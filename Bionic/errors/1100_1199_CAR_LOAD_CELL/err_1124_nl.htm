1124 E_LMS_REF_CAL_UNSUCCESSFUL

Summary

 This section describes the 1100_1199_CAR_LOAD_CELL error E_LMS_REF_CAL_UNSUCCESSFUL.

Description

The reference car load calibration has failed

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Wrong (e.g. too heavy) load in the car Ensure that the correct reference load is in the car
2 The measured values during calibration are instable Check general wiring to the car load measuring device.
Check for EMC disturbances.
3 Faulty reference car load value entered during the calibration procedure Enter the correct reference load during the calibration procedure (CF99)
4 The zero car load calibration is faulty Redo the zero car load calibration
5 The car load measuring device is mechanically bad adjusted Check mechanical fixation of the car load measuring device
6 Malfunction of the car load measuring device Replace the car load measuring device (e.g. CLC)
