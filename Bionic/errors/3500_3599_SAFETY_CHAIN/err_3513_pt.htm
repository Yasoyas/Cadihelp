3513 E_SAFETY_CHAIN_ERROR_NOT_RECOVERABLE

Summary

 This section describes the 3500_3599_SAFETY_CHAIN error E_SAFETY_CHAIN_ERROR_NOT_RECOVERABLE.

Description

The elevator wasn't able to recover from a opened safety circuit error (typ. after 20 repetitive door closing attempts). The elevator is blocked.
