3576 E_SAFETY_CIRCUIT_OPENED_AT_IKTS

Summary

 This section describes the 3500_3599_SAFETY_CHAIN error E_SAFETY_CIRCUIT_OPENED_AT_IKTS.

Description

Safety circuit opened unexpected (e.g. during trip) at tap 'IKTS'

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 At least one landing door has opened (e.g. KTS) Check why the corresponding door has opened
2 Safety circuit wiring faulty or safety circuit contact(s) defective or bad adjusted Check general wiring safety circuit
and safety circuit contacts at corresponding safety circuit section
