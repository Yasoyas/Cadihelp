3577 E_SAFETY_CIRCUIT_OPENED_AT_IHW

Summary

 This section describes the 3500_3599_SAFETY_CHAIN error E_SAFETY_CIRCUIT_OPENED_AT_IHW.

Description

Safety circuit opened unexpected (e.g. during trip) at tap 'IHW'

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 The car speed governor rope tension is too low (e.g. KSSBV) Check why the car speed governor rope tension is too low.
2 The pit ladder is not retracted Retract pit ladder
3 The car has exceeded the maximum speed limit (e.g. KBV) Check why the car has exceeded the maximum speed (ascending or descending overspeed).
Check log for possible previous reported messages.
Release elevator from blocked state (perform reset procedure).
4 The car has exceeded the hoistway end limit (e.g. KNE, KNE_U, KNE_D) Check why the car has exceeded the hoistway end limit.
Check log for possible previous reported messages.
Release elevator from blocked state (perform reset procedure).
5 Safety circuit wiring faulty or safety circuit contact(s) defective or bad adjusted Check general wiring safety circuit
and safety circuit contacts at corresponding safety circuit section
