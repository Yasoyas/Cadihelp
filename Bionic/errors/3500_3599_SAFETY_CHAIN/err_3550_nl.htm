3550 E_SAFETY_CIRCUIT_NOT_CLOSED_AT_ISK

Summary

 This section describes the 3500_3599_SAFETY_CHAIN error E_SAFETY_CIRCUIT_NOT_CLOSED_AT_ISK.

Description

Safety circuit not closed at tap 'ISK' when expected to be closed (e.g. before starting a trip)

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 At least one car door hasn't closed (e.g. KTC open) Check why the corresponding door hasn't closed
2 Safety circuit wiring faulty or safety circuit contact(s) defective or bad adjusted Check general wiring safety circuit
and safety circuit contacts at corresponding safety circuit section
