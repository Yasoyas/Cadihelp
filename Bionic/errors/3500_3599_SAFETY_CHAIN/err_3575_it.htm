3575 E_SAFETY_CIRCUIT_OPENED_AT_IUSK

Summary

 This section describes the 3500_3599_SAFETY_CHAIN error E_SAFETY_CIRCUIT_OPENED_AT_IUSK.

Description

Safety circuit opened unexpected (e.g. during trip) at tap 'IUSK'

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 The power supply of the safety circuit is faulty Check operation of power supply (e.g. fuses, input power etc.)
2 Earth fault Check safety circuit for short circuit to earth
