3516 E_SAFETY_CHAIN_KNA_ACTIVATION_CHECK_UNSUCCESSFUL

Summary

 This section describes the 3500_3599_SAFETY_CHAIN error E_SAFETY_CHAIN_KNA_ACTIVATION_CHECK_UNSUCCESSFUL.

Description

The periodic check if the overbridging of the safety circuit contact at the car emergency exit is running (e.g. used in fire operation Korea) has failed.
