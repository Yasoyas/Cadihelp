3509 E_SAFETY_CHAIN_SIM_DOORZONE_CONTACT_OVERBRIDGING_A

Summary

 This section describes the 3500_3599_SAFETY_CHAIN error E_SAFETY_CHAIN_SIM_DOORZONE_CONTACT_OVERBRIDGING_A.

Description

E_SAFETY_CHAIN_SIM_DOORZONE_CONTACT_OVERBRIDGING_ACTIVATION_UNSUCCESSFUL:

The overbridging of the door safety circuit contacts,
allowing to travel the car with opened doors (e.g. used in fire operation Korea) wasn't successful.

ndef

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Wiring of door zone simulation bad Check connections between SUET board and SIAP board
2 SIAP PCB defect (e.g. relays simulating door zone (PHS/PHS1) defect) Replace SIAP board
3 Door safety circuit overbridging failed Check errors 0338 for cause and actions
