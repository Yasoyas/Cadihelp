3574 E_SAFETY_CIRCUIT_OPENED_AT_IHC

Summary

 This section describes the 3500_3599_SAFETY_CHAIN error E_SAFETY_CIRCUIT_OPENED_AT_IHC.

Description

Safety circuit opened unexpected (e.g. during trip) at tap 'IHC'

Extra Info

Source ID

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 The car safety gear is engaged (e.g. KF) Release the car from engaged safety gear
