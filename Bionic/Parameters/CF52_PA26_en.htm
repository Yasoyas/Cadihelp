Parameter

 CF52_PA26.

DOOR_NO_VSTS_WHILE_SLOWCLOSING

In some cases door systems are unable to accept the signal
VRVRT to be active together with VST-S active during slow
closing of the door. In this case, for slow closing only the
signal VRVRT shall be used.
If this feature is set, and the door control software will
make shure that VST-S is never activated together with VRVRT.
