Description: Calculate M10 call cost.
Sent by the master to a lift to ask it to calculate its cost of
serving this specified M10 call. The lift sends the calculated
cost to the master using the telegram VXT_INT_GROUP / VXT_M10_COST.
The 'tel lift id' specify to which lift this order is given.
Call reference: reference given to the call by the master.
Over the hill enabled:
0: without 'over the hill'

1: with 'over the hill'

T_TERMINAL_POSITION

 1..max, position of the terminal where the M10 call is given.

The node_type and node_id are addresses used to identify the terminal where the M10 call has been generated.
