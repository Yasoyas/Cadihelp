STAT_LIFT (7)  /  M10_ALLOC_FEEDBACK (28)

Description

 This is the reply from the elevator to whom the call was allocated by the master. The 'master' sends the telegram mentioned in point 1. VXT_CMD_LIFT [group 3], VXT_M10_ALLOCATE_CALL [value 12], which is an allocation cum question to the best elevator. In the case that the state of the 'best' elevator has changed such that the allocation is 'not good' anymore, it replies with appropriate data in the telegram [i.e. VXT_ALLOC_REFUSED]. In case of 'good' allocation, it replies with data as VXT_ALLOC_ACCEPTED.

data_length: 2
data[0]: allocation id [0..255]
data[1]: VXT_T_ALLOC_FEEDBACK.

VXT_T_ALLOC_FEEDBACK:
0: VXT_ALLOC_REFUSED
1: VXT_ALLOC_ACCEPTED
