Description: M10 cost.

This is the answer of the lift to the request of the master to
calculate the cost of serving an M10 call (see telegram
VXT_INT_GROUP / VXT_CALC_M10_COST).
The 'call reference' is the reference that was given by the master to
identify the M10 call in the VXT_INT_GROUP / VXT_CALC_M10_COST
telegram (it allows the master to know if the received cost still
corresponds to the M10 call it is currently handling).

The 'tel lift id' specifies which lift calculated the cost.
The 'current halfround' is the halfround the lift is currently serving.
The 'allocated halfround' is the halfround where the M10 call can be
placed if allocated to this lift.
The 'over the hill' flag is 1 if the call to which this cost corresponds
causes an 'over the hill' trip (for itself or for another call), else 0.

'extra cost': 255: not usable (car is not at selector +/- 1 floor)
0..254: extra cost due to remaining approach time.
