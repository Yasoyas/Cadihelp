INT_LIFT (23)  /  CANCEL_CALLS (6)

Description

 attr_1 : vxt_side
attr_2 : vxt_dir
data_length: 2
data[0] : floor_number, 0 for all floors
data[1] : vxt_call_type

Description: Cancel lift calls or M10 calls.

Caution: only for the lift control, has no effect on the
group control. To cancel floor calls used the
telegram VXT_CANCEL_FLOOR_CALLS (only available

since Release 4.01).

vxt_call_type:
value for KS/KA/PI...
1 car call
2 allocated floor call (by lift control only)
3 simulated call
4 car and allocated floor call (by lift control only)
5 not usable for this telegram
6 car and simulated call (by lift control only)
7 simulated call and allocated floor

call (by lift control only)
8 all

value for M10
1 car call and M10 call with destination
at floor_number
2 M10 call with boarding at floor number
3 simulated call
4 car and M10 call with boarding or destination
at floor_number
5 not usable for this telegram
6 car call, simulated call and M10 call with

destination at floor_number
7 simulated call and M10 call with boarding
at floor_number
8 car call, simulated call, M10 call with boarding
or destination at floor_number

Notes for M10 only:
- attr_1 (side) is only relevant for car call. For M10
call it is always all sides.
- attr_2 (dir) is not used.

- floor_number zero and any call type with M10 call cancels
all M10 calls.
