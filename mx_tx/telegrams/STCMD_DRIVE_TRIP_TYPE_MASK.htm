STCMD_DRIVE (10)  /  TRIP_TYPE_MASK (1)

Description

 trip_type:
This flow describes a bit mask, which is used to block and release various drive trips. The mask is layed out as follows:

Bit_Nr: DEFINITION
------ -----------
data[1]:
0 Inspectiontrip
1 Learntrip
2 Synchtrip
3 Relevelling
4 Correctiontrip
5 Normaltrip
6 Commission
7 Takingtrip

data[0]
8 Mountingtrip
9 Recalltrip
10 Dockingtrip
11 - 15 Undefined Trip Types


When the bit is set to '1', then the trip is blocked and when the bit is '0',then the trip is allowed.If during a trip a new trip type mask is received,it will not be looked at until the trip is finished or in case of inspection or recall until the corresponding switch is deactivated.

Specification

Data[1]: T_VALUE

T_VALUE

 This Type is used for general data description.
