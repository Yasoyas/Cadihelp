Description: Destinations (exiting floor masks) are disabled (from
any source) or not (bit in exiting floor mask) from
boarding floor.
For M10 only. Floor numbers are in group notation
(1..max, 0 not allowed). For VCOM get only.
If data_length is 1 it means there are no destinations disabled

for the boarding floor (data[0]).
If there are no destinations disabled at all, only one telegram
with data_length at 0 is sent.
This telegram is also used to acknowledge destinations disable
changes instead of telegram VXT_STAT_M10_DISABLE_DEST when too
many of these telegrams would be necessary.

Exiting floor mask: bit at 1 for destination that is disabled, else at 0.

Example: data[0] = 3, data[1] = 24, data[2] = 2 => destinations 4, 5 and 10
are disabled from floor 3.
