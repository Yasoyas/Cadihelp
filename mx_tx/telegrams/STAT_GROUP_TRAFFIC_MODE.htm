Description: current traffic mode. Also current master overlay (to
be used by supervisory display)

vxt_trafficmode: VALUE DEFINITION
----- ------------
1 VXT_INTERFLOOR

2 VXT_UP_PEAK
3 VXT_DOWN_PEAK
4 (VXT_NOON_PEAK) not yet implemented
5 VXT_BLOCK_OPERATION

master_ovl_mode: 1 VXT_MASTER_NO_OVERLAY
2 VXT_MASTER_AITP
3 VXT_MASTER_M10
