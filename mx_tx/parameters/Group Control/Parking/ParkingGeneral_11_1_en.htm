Parking General

Summary

 This section describes the parameters in the parameter window Parking General of the parameter group Parking in subsystem Group Control.

Group Parking Mode

Flag to allow or not distribution of free cabins.

0: parking disabled (also for PI)
1: predefined parking enabled
2: statistical parking enabled (only for Miconic 10-GC)
3: predefined parking enabled with floor locked check (not for Tx4)
4: statistical parking enabled with floor locked check (only for Miconic 10-GC)

The parking mode with values 3 or 4 is CPU intensive. For the commission with such parking modes it is recommended to increase the value of the parameter P_STR_TIMEOUT_DISTR_FREE_CABIN to about 15s (150).

 














Lift Parking Mode


Parking mode for a car. Can be different for each car.

Mode:
 0: Parking off, no parking with this car
 1: Group parking, the car participates in the group parking (predefined
    or statistical).
 2: Back to main floor, the car returns to the main floor when
    it is free (without participating to group parking).





















Delay Car free for Parking


Time until the car is released for parking. If necessary the car is sent to the allocated parking zone. The timer starts when the doors are closed and the cabin has no destination. 
Occupied cars (car load exceeds predefined minimal load level) do not park.





















Distribution Free Cars


Time interval between two cycles of distribution of free cabins to parking zone.
