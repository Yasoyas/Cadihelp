Sabbath

Summary

 This section describes the parameters in the parameter window Sabbath of the parameter group Services in subsystem Lift Control.

Sabbath Start Floor

Defines the floor where the elevator parks after activation of input JSOP and before sabbath operation (SO) starts.

 

¨AD[ 02 02 0001 ]    * P_STR_SABBATH_START_FLOOR = 1

Sabbath Door Time Multiplier

Factor used to increase door open time(s) with sabbath operation.

The door open time is claculated:
Door open time = (door_exit_open_time) * (P_STR_SABBATH_DOORTIME_MULT) / 10

e.g. with P_STR_SABBATH_DOORTIME_MULT = 10 the factor for the door open time is 1.

 

¨AE[ 02 02 0001 ]    * P_STR_SABBATH_DOOR_TIME_MULT = 10

Sabbath Disable Relevelling

Disable relevelling trips for sabbath operation.

0 = Relevelling enabled in sabbath operation
1 = Relevelling disabled in sabbath operation

 

¨AF[ 02 02 0000 ]    * P_STR_SABBATH_DISABLE_RELEVEL = 0
