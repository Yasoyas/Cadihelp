Enforced Stop

Summary

 This section describes the parameters in the parameter window Enforced Stop of the parameter group Services in subsystem Lift Control.

Release Mode

For service JZH specify how the lift can be released after a forced stop at control floor.
Mode:
0: released manually
1: released after timeout
2: released manually or after timeout

 














Stop Floor


For the service JZH (Zwangshaltsteuerung) it specifies the control floor where the lift is forced to stop.





















Open Doors


For service JZH specify the side(s) which open(s) at the control floor.

door side mask:
+ 1 for side 1
+ 2 for side 20
+ 4 for side 30
+ 8 for side 40

for example:
side 1 and 20      = 1 + 2 = 3
side 1             = 1
side 1, 20 and 40  = 1 + 2 + 8 = 11





















Direction


For service JZH (Zwangshaltsteuerung) specify direction where the JZH must be controlled. 

Direction:
 0 = both
 1 = up
 2 = down





















Timeout


Defines the time to wait before the lift can leave the control floor with service 'forced stop' (active JZH).
