Building Sway

Summary

 This section describes the parameters in the parameter window Building Sway of the parameter group Services in subsystem Lift Control.

Low Sway Car Speed

Maximum car speed with active low sway (high wind) protection.

 














Medium Sway Car Speed


Maximum car speed with active medium sway (high wind) protection.





















Low / Medium Sway Parking Floor


Parking floor with active low or medium building sway (high wind). Floor where the car parks with active low (KGS1) or medium sway (KGS2).





















High Sway Recall Floor


Recall floor for building sway protection (high wind). Floor where passengers are taken to in order to leave the car.





















High Sway Parking Floor


Parking floor with active high building sway (high wind).





















Time  Sway Mode Off


Time delay for the deactivation of any sway mode (high sway off, medium sway off, low sway off)





















Restrict Serving Floor


Definition of floors not served under special circumstances or served only with additional constraints. (This parameter is a table with floor number as index)

Byte (Field) 1: Group floor number (index)
Byte (Field) 2: Serving of floor with building sway/high wind

Values for byte 2: 
0 = floor served with low and medium building sway
1 = floor locked (not served) with low and medium building sway
2 = floor locked (not served) with medium building sway only (served with low building sway)
3 = floor locked (not served) with low building sway only (served with medium building sway)
