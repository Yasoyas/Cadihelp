Handicapped Mode

Summary

 This section describes the parameters in the parameter window Handicapped Mode of the parameter group General in subsystem Lift Control.

Handicap Code Type

Defines the applicable handicapped code/norm for the elevator.

0: none
1: EN81-70 (Temporary)
2: Korea (KIS6831:2001)
3: EN81-70 (Permanent)

With M10 control refer also to the related parameters for handicapped operation e.g. P_M10C_CARS_FOR_HANDICAP

 














Handicapped Door Open


Door open time after serving of handicapped car and/or floor calls. Used for Handicapped Control. 

In order to extend the range for the door open times with handicapped control new parameters were introduced. This parameter is obsolete on software which supports the following parameters: 

Handicapped Door Open Time Car Call Side1
Handicapped Door Open Time Car Call Side2
Handicapped Door Open Time Floor Call Side1
Handicapped Door Open Time Floor Call Side2








Example: 10 s for handicapped car calls and 15 s for handicapped floor
         calls

 ¨52       *   PCT_GP_RS_TIMEOUT
   {...
    ¨96 [06 03 01 64 96] *   P_STR_TIMEOUT_HANDIC_DOOR_OPEN
   }

Default: 10 s for handicapped car and floor calls

 ¨52         *   PCT_GP_RS_TIMEOUT
   {...
   ¨96 [06 03 01 64 64]  * P_STR_TIMEOUT_HANDIC_DOOR_OPEN
   }













Handicapped Car Call Side 1


Door open time when a car call is served in handicapped mode. For side 1 only. Pressing the door close button closes the open door before the door open time has elapsed.





















Handicapped Floor Call Side 1


Door open time when a landing call is served in handicapped mode. For side 1 only. Pressing the door close button closes the open door before the door open time has elapsed.





















Handicapped Car Call Side 2


Door open time (side 20) when a car call is served in handicapped mode. Pressing the door close button closes the open door before the door open time has elapsed.





















Handicapped Floor Call Side 2


Door open time (side 20) when a landing call is served (boarding) in handicapped mode. Pressing the door close button closes the open door before the door open time has elapsed.
