Terminal Lift Allocation

Summary

 This section describes the parameters in the parameter window Terminal Lift Allocation of the parameter group General in subsystem Miconic 10.

Terminal/Lift Alloc.Priority

Defines the allocation priority for destination calls from each riser to each lift.

An M10 destination call entered from a terminal x is assigned according to the predefined priority defined for the lifts in the group (priority 1: highest, priority 4: lowest, priority 0: no allocation). With priority 0 a call from the respective riser will never be assigned to the lift (can be used for example when the walking time is too long).

 

Example: 3 riser, 5 cars.
   Riser 0: prio 1 for car 1 and 2 / prio 2 for car 3 / prio 3 for car 4 and 5
   Riser 1: prio 1 for car 3 / prio 2 for car 1, 2, 4 and 5
   Riser 2: prio 1 for car 4 and 5 / prio 2 for car 3 / prio 3 for car 1 and 2
   Other risers/lifts: default value 1
