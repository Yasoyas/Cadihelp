0003 NoKSE_STDSTL

Summary

 This section describes the Travel Control error NoKSE_STDSTL.

Description

The car stops at one of the shaft ends and detects that the KSE signal does not correspond to the position calculated by the distance tacho.

Extra Info

None.

Source ID

None.

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Incorrect parameter values Check that the physical position of the KSE magnets in the shaft is equal to the value(s) of the parameters 'KSE_DISTANCE' and 'SHAFT_PARA.GAP_LENGTH' (shaft parameters).
2 Incorrect magnet polarity Check the polarity of the magnets.
3 The lowest KSE magnet is a south.
4 The next magnet (lowest magnet of the gap) is a north.
5 The uppermost magnet is a south.
6 KSE Switch Check the KSE switch.
7 Check the connection from the KSE switch to the PCB.
