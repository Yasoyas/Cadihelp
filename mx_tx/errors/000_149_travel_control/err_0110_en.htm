0110 Motor Overload

Summary

 This section describes the Travel Control error Motor Overload.

Description

The motor overload in the linestarter has tripped, or the fault relay in the softstarter has tripped. (Hydro only)
On systems with Electronic Valve, this can also be caused by ETS overspeed opening SKD6.
