0052 OverflowCTP

Summary

 This section describes the Travel Control error OverflowCTP.

Description

The RAM area allocated for the calculation of the trip_plan is not large enough. For high rise buildings with high nominal speeds a large room area is needed, but even this will not be sufficient if extremely low acceleration and TERV parameters are set. Ensure that these parameters are set at between 300 and 2000 (mm/s2 and mm/s3).

Extra Info

None.

Source ID

None.
