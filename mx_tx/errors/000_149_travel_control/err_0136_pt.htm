0136 Safety Bypass

Summary

 This section describes the Travel Control error Safety Bypass.

Description

Error detected by the monitoring for the bypass circuit of the safety circuit. For Fire Recall and Firefighter Operation in Korea parts of the safety circuit (pit, emergency exit, and door contacts) is bridged by the control.

Extra Info

0x01: Status of relay RUESG does not match status of KUESG, or the feedback KUESG did not switch as expected within 2secs tolerance.
0x02: Status of relay RUENA does not match status of KUENA, or the feedback KUENA did not switch as expected within 2secs tolerance.
0x03: Status of relay RUESG does not match status of KUETFF, or the feedback KUETFF did not switch as expected within 2secs tolerance.
0x04: status of KUETFF does not match the supplementary input KUETFF1. Same for RUESG and RUENA.

Source ID

None.
