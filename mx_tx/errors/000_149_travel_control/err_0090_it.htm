0090 StopOverFault

Summary

 This section describes the Travel Control error StopOverFault.

Description

In the US and Canada, Firefighter Service (JBF) includes a relay which jumps out the stop switch inside the cab while the cars are being recalled to the lobby. Travel Control will report STOP_OVERRIDE_FAULT if it sees this relay go on when the car is not on fire recall service.
Comments: Not used.
