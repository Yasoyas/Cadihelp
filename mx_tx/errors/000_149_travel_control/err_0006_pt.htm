0006 SB_on_STDSTL

Summary

 This section describes the Travel Control error SB_on_STDSTL.

Description

The contactor SB is energised during standstill phase

Extra Info

None.

Source ID

None.

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Bad connection/wiring of the feedback contact Check the auxiliary contacts of SB and the wiring
2 Contactor SB moved manually
3 Dust in the control cabinet or other difficult environmental conditions causing conductivity problems. Remove dust.
