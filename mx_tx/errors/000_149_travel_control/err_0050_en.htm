0050 NoDistRelv

Summary

 This section describes the Travel Control error NoDistRelv.

Description

The software detects that the car is outside the relevel zone defined by the parameter 'Relevel Distance' during the preparation for a relevel trip. The software aborts the preparation for the relevel trip. In case the error occurs too frequent the elevator shuts down with fatal error. This situation may occur in situations where the car moves due to a high 'load change rate' when the car is loaded or unloaded on a floor. The same effect may be caused by high rope stretch/shrink when the car load changes (bouncing effects).

Extra Info

None.

Source ID

None.

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Wrong parameter values. Check the parameter for the relevelling zone 'Relevelling Distance'. 'Minimum Distance'
2 Excessive car movement at standstill. Check for causes of the car movement at standstill.
3 Inappropriate triggering of the relevelling trip. Check/adjust the parameter 'Relevelling delay'.
