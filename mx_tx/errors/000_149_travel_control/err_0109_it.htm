0109 KSE Failure

Summary

 This section describes the Travel Control error KSE Failure.

Description

The KSEU or KSED switch input disagrees with other inputs or with where car is meant to be in the hoistway. (Hydro only)

Extra Info

191 Hex (401 Dec): KSED says the car is at the bottom terminal, but the software thought it was at another floor.

192 Hex(402 Dec): KSEU says the car is at the top terminal, but the software thought it was at another floor.

193 Hex(403 Dec): The software thinks the car is at the bottom terminal, but KSED isn't open.

194 Hex(404 Dec): The software thinks the car is at the top terminal, but KSED is open.

195 Hex(405 Dec): The car is in the top terminal, but KSEU, ETSL1, and ETSL2 don't all agree with eachother. On a Dynahyd, ETSL3 and ETSL4 must also agree.
