0113 Thermistor Failure

Summary

 This section describes the Travel Control error Thermistor Failure.

Description

Occurs when the software detects that the thermistor for sensing the oil temperature has failed. (Hydro only)

Extra Info

x65 (hex)/101 (dec): The reading is out of range. Thermistor (Analog Oil Temp Sensor) not detected or bad thermistor.
xC9 (hex)/201 (dec): The oil is still cold after 10 attempts to heat it with bypass operation.
x12D (hex)/301 (dec): Oil temperature has reached 62 degrees and was in the hot oil temperature range before (> 55 degrees). Motor can not shutdown dispatch immediately.
x191 (hex)/401 (dec): Oil temperature has jumped more than +/- 5 degrees within 1 second. Check temp sensor / oil level

Source ID

None.

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Broken sensor replace sensor.
2 Low oil level refill oil.
