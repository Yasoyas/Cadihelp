0103 HighSpeedStop

Summary

 This section describes the Travel Control error HighSpeedStop.

Description

For SSDC drives only: Emergency stop when the car was moving with more than 0.75 m/s (150 FPM). After emergency the dymanic braking resistors will heat up from the kinetic energy depending on the speed. With this event the car is blocked for a short time after too many emergency stops within a short period of time in order to prevent the resistors from overheating.
