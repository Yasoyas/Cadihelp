0094 DoorLockFault

Summary

 This section describes the Travel Control error DoorLockFault.

Description

The safety circuit shows closed shaft or car door (taps ISKT4 or ISKT5 on) while the door status is 'open'.

Comments: The door state (open) must match with the information (KTS and KTC) read from the safety circuit.
