0004 InvldMovement

Summary

 This section describes the Travel Control error InvldMovement.

Description

Invalid movement. A movement of the car was detected while Travel Control is in standstill phase.The event is logged when the car starts to move after the brake is opened manually and Travel Control is able to detect the movement.

Extra Info

None.

Source ID

None.

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Defective drive brake or bad brake adjustment (brake torque not sufficient to hold car at standstill) Check the mechanical brake ( Adjustment of brake , possible wear of brake lining ). Brake torque sufficient to hold fully loaded or empty car.
2 Verify brake action by performing an emergency stop at full speed in up direction with empty car and check if the car comes to rest. Use brake test to measure average deceleration after emergency stop. Be aware of possible rope slip.
