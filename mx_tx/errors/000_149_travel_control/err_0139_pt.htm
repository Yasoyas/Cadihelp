0139 Unintended Car Movement

Summary

 This section describes the Travel Control error Unintended Car Movement.

Description

Mosis detects an unintended car movement. The unintended movement detection device opens the safety circuit. No car movement is allowed until the unintended movement detection device is manually reset. Unintended movement protection device is required by the following codes: A17.1 / Hong Kong Code of Practice (Amendm.10) / Korean Code. (list subject to change)

The error is also logged when the acceptance test for unintended movement is performed. The limits for the detection of unintended movement depend on the detection device. With MoSIS the limits are: distance from floor > 100mm or speed >0.3m/s.
