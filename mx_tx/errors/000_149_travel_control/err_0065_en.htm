0065 PHNR_D_Fault

Summary

 This section describes the Travel Control error PHNR_D_Fault.

Description

The upper photocell (PHNR_D) is not working properly.
Comments: The car left the fin but the PHNR_D still reports IN_FIN, or the car is at floor level and PHNR_D still reports OUT_FIN, or two PHNR_D signals are received too close together which means that the PHNR_D signal is bouncing.
Comment: This error does not trigger an emergency stop. An error detected during the trip is logged only when the car has stopped. Therefore the trip-phase is always logged as STANDSTILL.

Extra Info

None.

Source ID

None.

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Check the PHNR_D connection from the photocell to the PCB.
2 Check for undisturbed function of the photocell: dirt, holes in the fins (for attaching a rope) not sealed so that the photocell detects it, the rope between fins, horizontal distance beween fin and photocell.
3 Check the ground connection on top of the car and to the machine room.
