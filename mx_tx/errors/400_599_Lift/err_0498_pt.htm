0498 Group 24VDC Fail

Summary

 This section describes the Lift error Group 24VDC Fail.

Description

The Group 24V supply voltage is out of tolerance (24V20%). If the warning 'Lift24VDCFail' occurs simultaneously, the system may be running on the backup battery power only.

Note: The battery charge level is tested every hour and after a manual reset. After either 'Lift24VDCFail' or 'Group24VDCFail' event the battery is not tested, since testing the battery could eventually interrupt power to the control.
