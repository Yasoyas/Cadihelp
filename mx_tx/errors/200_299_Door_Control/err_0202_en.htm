0202 DoorOperErr

Summary

 This section describes the Door Control error DoorOperErr.

Description

Door operator error. Error detected on the door drive behaviour by door control.
The door can not reach its end position. After several attempts the door will be blocked.

Extra Info

01: KET-O and KET-S are active at the same time (illegal status)
02: KET-S does not switch when expected
03: KET-O does not switch when expected
04. Output VST-S is faulty or has short circuit.
05: Output VST-O is faulty or has short circuit.
06: The door drive does not move the door, although VST-O / VST-S is active.
07: For manually-operated or semi-automatic doors the RTS-feedabck (shaft door closed) is not active after a close command has been given. Either the shaft door is open or the shaft door contact (RTS) is faulty.
08: Door motor moves the door in the wrong direction

Source ID

None.

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Extra info 1,2,3 Check the door contacts KET-O, KET-S and the related wiring
2 Check if the end position can be reached mechanically
3 Check for dirty door guides.
4 Extra info 4,5 Check the connections to the door drive
5 Replace the interface board
6 Extra info 6 Check the door drive board and connections (fuse and power supply).
7 Check the physical door parts (V-belt)
8 Extra info 7 For manually-operated or semi-automatic doors check if the landing door is closed.
9 Check the landing door contacts and wiring.
10 Extra info 8 Check if electrical phase sequence is wrong.
11 Check if VST-O/VST-S or wiring is mixed up
