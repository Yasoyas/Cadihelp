0172 InvDriveParam

Summary

 This section describes the Variodyn E error InvDriveParam.

Description

Parameter values stored in the frequency converter (BARMAG ER113) do not correspond with their equivalents in the control software (inconsistence)

Extra Info

frequency converter parameter ID: 0x01: FN;
0x02: VKN;
0x03: MM_IMP;
0x04: S_KBR_2;
0x05: FT_RU1;
0x06: DELTA_U;
0x07: FC_MAX_R;
0x08: T_DIFF;
0x09: T_AUF;
0x0A: U_END;

Source ID

None.

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Communication PGO frequency converter not successful/impossible Check RS232 connection PGO frequency converter
2 Communication PGO frequency converter had been interrupted Perform double-reset on PGO and reset on frequency converter, wait approx. 5 minutes (!)
