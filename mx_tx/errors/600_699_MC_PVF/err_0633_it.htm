0633 HW encoder warning

Summary

 This section describes the MC PVF error HW encoder warning.

Description

Motor tacho warning: The signals A, A-, B, B- from the motor tacho are proved by the PVF HW during stand still and during trip. If a failure is detected at trip start, an error is generated and the start is not allowed. During a trip, each time the failure is detected a warning is generated. In such case, the consecutive warning may be error 614.

Extra Info

None.

Source ID

None.

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 Bad tacho electronic or tacho cable (connector is not inserted properly or damaged cable) Check motor tacho, cable and connectors (PVF X9 and/or IG connector).
2 Disturbance or interference. Check tacho shielding.
3 Mechanical vibrations of the tacho plate at the beginning and the end of a trip.
