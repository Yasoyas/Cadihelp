1203 ExtReset

Summary

 This section describes the LON error ExtReset.

Description

Generated when SIOL or SIOG is reset by an external cause. (SIOL/SIOG ONLY)
TX5 reports either CPU errors 1000 ... 1100 or 403 instead.

Extra Info

None.

Source ID

SIOL or SIOG

Causes and Actions
ID Possible causes of message Actions to be taken
1 RESET button pressed No action required
2 EMC problems Close the AS1 cabinet doors.
3 Check for strong EMC source in the building.
4 Check that the 24V power supply is separated from higher voltage cables, for example, the power supply cables of the brake.
5 Make sure that the SIO ground and mass connections are properly done.
6 Check all of the ground and mass connections in the cabinet
7 Voltage monitor Check +24V supply voltage
8 Check for loose wiring
9 Defective SIO board Replace SIO board.
