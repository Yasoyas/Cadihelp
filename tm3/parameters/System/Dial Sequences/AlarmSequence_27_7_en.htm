Alarm Sequence

Summary

 This section describes the parameters in the parameter window Alarm Sequence of the parameter group Dial Sequences in subsystem System.

Alarm Sequence

Sequence of telephone numbers the TM3 module tries to connect in case of an alarm in the elevator.
Index: not used and not configurable
The sequence numbers rely on the telephone numbers defined in the parameter Phone number section.
even in TM3-Mode you can call to a normal telephone. If you specify a phone number which ends with *9, a connection like in TAM Mode is then established and the speaker and microphone in the car are automaticaly enabled.
The alarm can be acknowledged by the receiveing phone sending DTMF commands (see TM2-TAM Documentation).
It's a good idea to define such a telephone number as the last number in the alarm sequence!
